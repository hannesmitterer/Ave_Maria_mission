SITUATION ANALYSIS Ave Maria Mission remains the primary stabilizer in a region currently devoid of major NGO presence (WFP, UNICEF absent). Following recent clashes, the mission is now hosting 7,326 internally displaced persons (IDPs) directly on mission grounds, with an additional ~3,000 in the immediate host community. 2. PROBLEM STATEMENT Our existing water infrastructure was designed for a quiet parish community. It is now supporting over 10,000 lives.
