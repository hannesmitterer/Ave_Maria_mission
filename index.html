Mission Overview

The Ave Maria Mission serves over 10,300 displaced individuals in the Yambio Region, South Sudan. With major NGOs absent, this mission is providing emergency support, clean water, and education to those in dire need.

Critical Issues

Water Crisis

Current water infrastructure is overwhelmed due to a 300% increase in population. Over 10,000 people are at risk. Immediate funding required for a new borehole: $18,500.

Education Emergency

1,027 children are in urgent need of classrooms. Previously, only 360 students could be accommodated. Deadline: February 2026 for the start of the academic year.

About Us

Ave Maria Mission is a beacon of hope for internally displaced persons. Our services include clean water, educational support, and humanitarian aid to sustain life and rebuild futures in a region affected by crisis.
